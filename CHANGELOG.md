# CHANGELOG

This is the changelog for the project 'analytical_budget_tracker', all releases will be documented here

## **v1.0-beta**

### **[2026-05-03]: Analytical Budget Tracker Beta Release**:

**v1.0-beta marks the transition to a fully persistent, cross-platform budget tracker with full analysis.**

#### Features:

##### Budget Input

- The ability to record:
- Name
- Currency
- Budget Date
- The month and year of which you are recording your budget
- Income Name and Amount
- Expense Name and Amount

##### Budget Tables

- The ability to view, and delete your budget in one income and expense tables, or list views
- The ability to import and save income and expense items with strict validation

##### Budget Analysis

- The ability to analyze the following in the form of donut charts:
    - Income Distribution: Shows all your income sources in a chart
    - Expense Distribution: Shows all your expenses in a chart
    - Income and Expense Distribution: Shows your total income and total expenses in a chart
- The ability to analyze the following in the form of cards
    - Average Income and Expense
    - Lowest Income and Expense amount
    - Highest Income and Expense amount)
- Verdict
    - Total Income
    - Total Expenses
    - Take-home: Income - Expenses
    - Verdict
        - SURPLUS: If take-home is higher than 0
        - BREAKTHROUGH: If take-home is equal to 0
        - DEFICIT: If take-home is less than 0

##### Other

- Persistence that saves all the data you have entered throughout the app
